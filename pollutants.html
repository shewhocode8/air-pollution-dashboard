Detailed information on specific pollutants and their concentrations:

Pollutant Description Health Effects Typical Sources

PM10 Particulate matter less than 10 micrometers in diameter Can affect lung function and lead to respiratory issues Construction sites, road dust
NO2 Nitrogen dioxide Can irritate airways and decrease lung function Vehicle emissions, power plants
O3 Ozone Can cause respiratory problems and worsen asthma Vehicle emissions, industrial processes
